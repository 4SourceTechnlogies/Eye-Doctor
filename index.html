About Us

Eye Doctors institute

EYE DOCTORS, a Day-care Eye Institute and Surgery Centre, is proud to offer state of art technologies, panel of talented board certified doctors and customized care to the patients. All doctors are committed to excellence in standard of care they contribute to their respective fields. Highly skilled staff is trained not only to address basic vision care needs but also to provide sophisticated treatment for various eye conditions so that patientsreceive comprehensive treatment options. It possesses latest equipments for examination, investigations, medical and surgical treatment.All types of lamellar and penetrating corneal transplantations, keratoprosthesis, cataract, squint, glaucoma and oculoplasty surgeries will be done here. Patients are looked after by an extremely efficient staff and guided throughout your experience in the institute with utmost care. Hospital visit can be very stressful for the patient. They should feel positive and relaxed in the peaceful and relaxing environment. Keeping this in mind, the hospital has been very meticulously designed by the director herself looking after even the minutest of details.
